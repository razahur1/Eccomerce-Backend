SIYAH

At SIYAH, we are committed to providing you with a seamless shopping experience. Our policies are designed to ensure that you have a clear understanding of our processes and practices. Please review the following policies to help guide your shopping experience with us.

Returns and Exchanges

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you need to return or exchange an item, please follow these guidelines: Eligibility: Items must be returned within [number] days of receipt and must be in their original condition, including tags and packaging. Process: To initiate a return or exchange, contact our customer service team at siyahofficial@gmail.com or 03253985681 for instructions. Refunds: Once your return is received and inspected, a refund will be processed to your original payment method within 30 days. Shipping costs are non-refundable.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us: Remember Your Preferences: Cookies allow us to remember your preferences and settings, making your experience more personalized and convenient during future visits. Improve Website Functionality: By analyzing how you interact with our site, cookies help us identify areas for improvement and ensure that our website performs optimally. Enhance Security: Cookies can help us protect your account and transaction details, ensuring a secure shopping experience.

Web Beacons

Track Website Usage: Web beacons allow us to track user behavior and website interactions, helping us understand how our website is used and identify areas for improvement. Measure Email Campaign Effectiveness: When included in emails, web beacons help us gauge the success of our email campaigns by tracking open rates and user engagement.

Third-Party Services

We may also use third-party services that employ cookies and web beacons to assist us in analyzing website usage and providing targeted advertisements. These third-party services have their own privacy policies and practices, and we encourage you to review them.

Need Any Helps?

Contact us and we’ll get back to you as soon as we can.
